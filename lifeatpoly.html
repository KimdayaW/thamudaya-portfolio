Why should we join CCA?

Is it important to join a CCA?
CCA plays a huge part in enriching the students' lives outside classroom. Investing in CCAs is important as it can help children connect to different communities, form different groups of friends, and build relationships that go way beyond the classroom, giving them a good social circle, while keeping them mentally and emotionally healthy.

MY CCA

Life Saver

SP Lifesaving club(SPLS)
Lifesaving is not a sport nor a recreational activity. It is a lifelong skill that is rewarding and necessary. We teach the life skills that arm you with the training and knowledge to save a life in a crisis situation. We are a team whose focus is not on trophies and medals but on human relations and life.

Videography Club

SP Videography Club(SPVC)
Interested in making videos, telling stories or picking up a new skill? Video Graphy Club wants you to join us in making some classic movie magic.The Club have a lot of competitions and challenging events welcoming for all the group members .

Stage Management

SP Stage Management(SPSM)
SP Stage Management is a dedicated and passionate crew specialising in providing sound and lights for events. The club aims to expand the knowledge of each member through trainings in sound engineering and technical light play.

Infocomm Club

SP Infocomm (SPIC)
Interested in coding, programming and everything related to the fascinating world of bits and bytes? SP Infocomm (SPIC) is for you! Covering an exciting range of activities such as online competitions, group bonding events, and coding workshops, we've got it all!
